More about JP Performance Cars

We are a Ferrari, Maserati and Lamborghini specialist based in Burntwood, Staffordshire. We are conveniently located only 3 miles from Junction T6 of the M6 Toll. Owners of Ferrari, Maserati and Lamborghini vehicles know how special their cars are and that they need special care. Our highly trained and skilled team have over 46 years of collective experience of these marques, meaning we have the necessary experience to maintain your car to the highest standard. You will find our workshop is fully equipped with all the necessary diagnostic systems and tools. We may not be the biggest specialist, nor have the fanciest premises, but we continually strive to be the best. We specialise in servicing, repairs and vehicle sales, but our experience runs from interior restoration , to finding solutions for difficult to fix problems and full engine rebuilds. We offer fixed price servicing to give you peace of mind that you’ll always know what your bill will be. If, however, unforeseen work is required, we will never undertake work without approval, nor will we needlessly try to upsell unnecessary work. Please have a good look around our website to see what services we provide. We are always glad to welcome people into the workshop, so why not pop in to see what we are about and discuss your car
